PAPER TIGER CO-FOUNDERS

Paper Tiger was founded in 2007 out of a desire to solve problems through design. We cut our teeth working in the music industry for 10 years before we started this company and it was like boot camp for running an agency. We learned about creative personality types, tight deadlines, shoestring budgets, and DIY work ethics. 

In 2007, everyone was thinking outside-the-box. We noticed the box was empty and moved right in. Now we occupy the whole thing with ideas so practical that they push creative boundries.
